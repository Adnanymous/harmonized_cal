Frequently Asked Questions

Why start on Tuesday?

The Tuesday start aligns with ancient wisdom and creates a balanced weekly cycle that maintains consistency year after year.

What are the special days?

The "Day Out of Time" (March 19) and Leap Day (March 18) exist outside the weekly cycle, allowing for perfect calendar alignment while maintaining natural timekeeping.

How does it handle leap years?

Leap years add an extra day (March 18) while maintaining the weekly cycle, ensuring perfect alignment with astronomical events.
